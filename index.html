About Us

Yummy is designed to streamline restaurant operations and enhance customer experiences. Our platform provides a comprehensive solution for managing orders, users, and categories, ensuring that your restaurant runs smoothly and efficiently.

The inspiration for Yummy came from my own experiences working in the restaurant industry. I saw firsthand how challenging it can be to manage orders, track inventory, and ensure customer satisfaction. This project is a culmination of my passion for technology and my desire to make restaurant management more efficient and enjoyable. This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
